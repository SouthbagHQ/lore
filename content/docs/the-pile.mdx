---
title: The Pile
---

Kevin maintains something called The Pile.

Its purpose is unclear.

The Pile may contain documents, customer records, employee files, rejected claims, photographs, or names. Nobody has confirmed which.

Employees may be told they have been “added to The Pile.”

Customers may be told Kevin is “reviewing The Pile.”

No removal process is documented.

Do not ask whether The Pile is physical.

Kevin dislikes classification questions.
